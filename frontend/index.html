SUGARCORE X

SUGARCORE X is an event-driven w3 community for anime fans, vtubers, cosplayers, artists and musicians. We're here to feature unrecognized talents and unite communities together through anime conventions, exclusive japan-themed events/gigs and provide recreational perks to all who loves the anime culture.

SUGARCORE X Non-Fungible Tokens will be the holder's all access pass to an entire year of gaming/cosplay events, exclusive SFW/NSFW auditory and visual art space, exclusive VIP parties (for holders age 18+ only) and DJ/music events. Owning an SX NFT will give the holder eligibility to purchase an SX 3D avatar that is compatible to all Unity-made VR space.

During public sale, event-based perks are already active for SX owners. Updates of partnered anime events and VR online events and schedules are going to be posted in the Discord (dsc.gg/sugarcorex) server. The exclusive auditory + visual art space will be completed 2-7 weeks after NFT public sale. All visual and audio materials presented in the SX Art Space are free to be used by the SX holders.
